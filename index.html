Our Journey

Originating from a spirited family with over 25 years in the tequila industry, El Sativo is a sipping waterfall of stone fruits and amazing notes found only in the lowlands of Amititan, Jalisco, known as the sacred grounds of Tequila.  A Single Estate masterpiece and the winner of two 2020 double gold awards, including Tequila of the Year (San Francisco Spirits World Competition).

El Sativo sets itself apart by utilizing a special distillation method that naturally highlights the Agave plant’s precious resources known to have Mood Lifting and Energy Boosting properties that exemplify #SoSativo.

We are proud to be OK Kosher certified and our bottles are 100% Smart Combustible Recycled Glass with Organic Soluble Labels. Every Bottle Sold Gives Back To Ocean Conservancy.

Truly "A Better Tequila for a Better World."

100% "Single Estate" Agave

Single Estate grown on the Volcanic Belt, our agaves are planted, nurtured, and harvested solely in our fields and never sourced.  The agave shortage has hit Mexico hard yet we’ve been able to flourish due to our all organic approach and practices.

El Sativo Tequila is certified organic by the USDA and EU, 100% Non–GMO and Certified Kosher by Rabbinical Law.

We cut no corners

Cultivation is carried out without pesticides or fungicides. Only organic fertilizers are used. The organic certification process is vital, complex and time consuming because the process is different from common tequila. One sip and you will understand why we go through the extra efforts.

Cooking, filtration & fermentation

We slowly steam our blue agaves for 24-48 hours in our small batch stone ovens. No autoclaves. No diffusers. Fermenting with our environmentally occurring yeast; El Sativo spring water is naturally purified through 135 feet of volcanic rock and then RO filtered, which yields our perfect PH and some of the cleanest water on the planet.

One of a kind innovation

There are naturally occurring plant benefits found in the agave known to boost energy and mood while reducing inflammation for a much better drinking experience. We pull out these particular raw ingredients prior to cooking and maintain until distillation. This improves the molecular structure, a marvel of stimulated tequila.

Sustainability & future forward

The El Sativo family believes the key to a better tomorrow is sustainability. With the future in our hands we will continue to move forward as we expand and build a new distillery powered by solar and green energy. We recycle and upcycle material, compost, source locally, and produce organically because we truly care about "A Better Tequila and a Better World."
